💡 Offensive vs. Defensive Sectors — Read This First!

🏈 Offensive (Growth)

Technology, Consumer Disc., Financials, Energy, Industrials, Materials — these shine when the economy is growing but crash harder in recessions. Higher beta means bigger swings both ways. PEs get expensive when everyone is buying.

🛡️ Defensive (Safety)

Consumer Staples, Utilities, Healthcare, Telecom — people need groceries, electricity, medicine, and phone service no matter what the economy does. Lower beta, steadier dividends, and usually cheaper PEs during a boom.
